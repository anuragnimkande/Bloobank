Benefits of Blood Donation

Free health checkup In order to give blood, you are required to undergo a health screening. A trained staff member performs this checkup. They will check your: 1)pulse 2)blood pressure 3)body temperature 4)hemoglobin levels This free mini-physical can offer excellent insight into your health

ANTI-CANCER BENEFITS

Blood donation helps in lowering the risk of cancer. By donating blood the iron stores in the body are maintained at healthy levels. A reduction in the iron level in the body is linked with low cancer risk

A HEALTHIER HEART AND VASCULAR SYSTEM

Regular blood donation is linked to lower blood pressure and a lower risk for heart attacks. It definitely helps to reduce cardiovascular risk factors, says Dr. DeSimone.

STIMULATES BLOOD CELL PRODUCTION

After donating blood, the body works to replenish the blood loss. This stimulates the production of new blood cells and in turn, helps in maintaining good health
